WHO I AM

I took my first breath on the 15th of april 1994 only weighing at 4 pounds! I never knew my true potential in life before the age of 12. After I created my first Piczo website, that’s when everything unfolded. My skills and love for designing website grew so fast I always wanted to learn more and do more. At the age of 15 I had my very own sub-domain which I had more fun re-designing the template from scratch then actually blogging. After playing with codes I wanted to expand my knowledge with Corel Paint Shop Pro and then went to Adobe Photoshop. I knew from then what I wanted to do. Become a Graphic Designer. Now I spend my days designing and taking care of my puppy and my cat who fill my heart with joy when ever something is going wrong.

WHAT I LOVE

Web Design is my first love. I've been designing websites from the age of 12, I've grown a passion to create beautiful simplistic website. I love the thrill of breaking code and finding the solution to it. My goal designing any website is to make it one-of-a-kind with sleek design and functionality.

Print / Magazines, I've learned the beauty of printing magazines and designing them through school. I admire and appreciate cook books so much, as well as any magazine I may stumble upon. I have a passion on creating legible layout with a modern bold design.

Posters & Stationaries are such a joy to work with. The best part is seeing the end result through the audience. I adore seeing the process on paper and the clients reaction to the progress we're doing to deliver.
